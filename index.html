Bio

Hello! I am Jesus Diaz Sanchez, a researcher in AI and molecular sciences, currently completing my Ph.D. at MIT with Prof. Rafael Gomez-Bombarelli, Prof. Connor Coley.

My current research focuses on building agentic systems for next-gen functional molecules and materials at scale, with applications in drug discovery, sustainability, and more.

I am grateful to have my research supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. You can find more information about my research, publications, and projects, as well as my professional and academic background below.
