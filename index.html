Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at Eric and Wendy Schmidt Center of Broad Institute. I finished my Ph.D. in MIT CSAIL, advised by Regina Barzilay and Tommi Jaakkola . My research seeks to develop novel machine learning algorithms for biology, including drug discovery, immunology, genetic engineering, and synthetic biology. I am particularly interested in deep generative models and graph neural networks.
