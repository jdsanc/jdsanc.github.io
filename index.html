Bio

Hello! I am Wenhao Gao (高文昊). I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at MIT, advised by Connor W. Coley. My research focueses on accelerating the molecular discovery processes with AI and robotics. Eventually, I want to accomplish an idea called autonomous molecular discovery, which is an systematic methodology for molecular discovery that require no or little human input and thus make discovery processes scalable. My previous work has focused on developing AI algorithms with broader practical applicability for designing small molecules, expanding upon the synthetic accessibility and sample efficiency.
